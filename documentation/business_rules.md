# Business Rules

## Revenue Definition

Revenue will be calculated using only transactions with:

* Complete
* Shipped

Statuses excluded:

* Cancelled
* Returned
* Processing

Reason:
These transactions do not represent finalized sales.

---

## Customer Definition

Customer = Unique user_id

---

## Order Definition

Order = Unique order_id

---

## Average Order Value (AOV)

AOV = Revenue / Orders

---

## Product Revenue

Revenue attributed to the product purchased in order_items.

---

## Category Revenue

Revenue aggregated by product category.

---

## Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Total revenue generated by a customer across all completed purchases.
